About the Artist

painted prose is my archive of ache and wonder — a place where brush and language share the same heartbeat.

I'm Mezziah, an illustrator and poet collecting field notes on tenderness, decay, and the peculiar ways light finds us.

This site is an illustrated diary: blush-pink, vintage-academic, with a whisper of the gothic.

“Every line I draw is a sentence I could not speak.”
